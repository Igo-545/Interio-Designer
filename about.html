HI, I'm Oksana

Otchik interior

Designer

I have been working in interior design for about 5 years. I started my career with training in 3D visualization and modeling in Moscow 3d modelling school and then the interior design school of Masha Chernaya. I worked in the interior design studio Soffit Interiors, and also collaborated with the British company Victoria Plum as a designer-installer. At the moment I am engaged in the creation of design projects for residential real estate from the development of concepts and 3D visualization to the full implementation of the project with the author's supervision. Depending on the needs and wishes of the customer, you can separately use one or several types of services, for example, 3D visualization or the creation of an exclusively technical part, which includes the creation of an optimal layout solution and a package of drawings for builders. At each stage of my work I set myself the goal of helping the client to use the space as efficiently as possible, make it aesthetically and tactilely pleasant.
