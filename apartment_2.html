Oksana otchik

Apartment

in Cyprus

01

Guest room

The total area of ​​the apartment is 102 sq.m. The layout of the apartment is simple and logical, designed for a family with two children. The main part traditionally contains an entrance hall, living room and kitchen.

The apartment's windows overlook a gorgeous landscape, and for preserving this natural potential, a calm design was developed in light neutral shades.

02

Materials

The budget allowed using high quality, expensive furniture. For the living room was chosen lounge chair from Møbel Copenhagen. The font dining armchair with matte black frame is designed by David Thulstrup. The design language is neutral enough for it to be placed into various environments and contexts, without taking all the attention. For lighting, we used rounded minimalistic shapes: the Laurent Collection pendant with two milk globes by Lambertetfils, Volker Haug Studio Anton wall lights. Decor objects: cute Kay Bojesen Puffins, vintage Antonio da Ros Vases, T Vases by Ligne-roset.

03

Kitchen

The U-shaped kitchen became the most ergonomic solution and allowed to place semi-bar leather chairs and a side niche for decoration objects. MDF facades veneered with dark walnut were chosen, as well as a built-in niche in the hallway. Dark wooden facades perfectly match the gray stone of the countertop and white marble porcelain stoneware on the floor.

04

Bedroom

The bedrooms and bathroom are separated from the common space by a door. Our challenge was to organize the space not only functionally and ergonomically, but also to design the interior in an aesthetically minimalistic way.

05

Bathroom

The theme of the white marble floor was continued in the bathroom at the customer's request. In combination with a hexagonal mosaic of grey stone, a grey wall-hung cabinet and black mixer taps, a minimalist, pleasant space for relaxation was created.
