Oksana otchik

Apartment

in Kirov

01

Guest room

For the living room, a solution with rounded wall corners and streamlined furniture was chosen. The owners of the apartment love art, so they supported the idea of ​​paintings and decorated the walls of the living room and bedroom with huge modern abstractions by Hiroyuki Hamada.

02

Materials

Rounded smooth lines in the interior are supported by rounded pieces of furniture. The Stump Table by Faye Toogood is understated, elegant, and heroic in its generous proportions. With stocky forms rendered in warm textural fiberboard, the table feel organic and elemental. Crisp lines, graphic shapes, and a glossy lacquered finish lend this object sophistication too. The Stump Coffee Tables is ideal as a sculptural center point around which living room was arranged.

03

Kitchen

Request from a customer was for a bright and cozy home for a family with little children. An essential requirement was a bar table with open access from the corridor side, where the customer could meet clients, sign documents and view catalogs. Due to this a niche for the refrigerator and built-in appliances, as well as a small bar table with storage below was appeared.

04

Bedroom

Decorative plaster in a milky shade was used for the walls, limestone porcelain tiles, and natural oak flooring. To create contrast, dark lamps, furniture elements, and contrasting paintings were used pointwise.

05

Bathroom

The theme of the white marble floor was continued in the bathroom at the customer's request. In combination with a hexagonal mosaic of grey stone, a grey wall-hung cabinet and black mixer taps, a minimalist, pleasant space for relaxation was created.
